About This Workbook

A compelling Case for Support is the foundation of successful fundraising in 2025. This workbook guides you through a strategic, equity-centered process to articulate your organization's value proposition, demonstrate measurable impact, and inspire philanthropic investment from diverse donor audiences.

2025 Focus Areas: This edition emphasizes trust-based philanthropy, community-centric approaches, diversity/equity/inclusion integration, data storytelling, digital engagement strategies, and multi-generational donor communication.
